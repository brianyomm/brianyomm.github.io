ABOUT

I'm a designer of many disciplines, from industrial, fashion, to web. I have immersed myself in different fields to channel my creativity. I strongly believe that creativity is not linear and moments of insight can come from anyone, anywhere, and anytime.

I trained as an industrial designer at Rhode Island School of Design. Essentially my training involved creating objects around a piece of technology. During my transition to my professional career, I noticed a radical change emerging that college did not prepare us for. Tech companies started asking designers to create products that the end user experienced primarily through software. UI, UX, ID had to intertwine in a cohesive manner to produce a product. This is when I decided to shift gears to develop new skills and adopted a new profession. I call it technology design. As a technology designer we must create objects that users can use physically and digitally in harmony.
